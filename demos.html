Inline Equations

When $a \ne 0$, there are two solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) and they are

$$ x = {-b \pm \sqrt{b^2-4ac} \over 2a}. $$

当 $a \ne 0$时，方程 \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) 有两个根：

$$ x=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{\Delta }}{2a}=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^{2}-4ac }}{2a} $$
